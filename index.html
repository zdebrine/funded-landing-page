Proximity SDK

The real world as your interface

Your mobile app can easily get lost in the sea of applications on a user's device. Cut through that noise by providing your users with contextually driven experiences based on their location or the objects around them.
  • Geofences
  • Beacons
A geofence is a virtual geographic boundary in the physical world. When one of your app users enters the location of the geofence, you can use that event to create marketing campaigns, trigger a new feature in your app, or simply log the visit to see how often your users go to that location.
Explore geofencing
Beacons are small devices that broadcast a Bluetooth signal. Smartphones can detect this signal and use it to create marketing campaigns, trigger a new feature in your app, or simply log the visit to see how often your users go to that location. Beacons are usually used inside where GPS signals can't reach.
Explore beacons
